Our Story

How Destiny Found Love Through An Arranged Marriage

What began as a simple introduction between two families slowly blossomed into something far more meaningful. In the quiet moments of conversation and shared laughter, two people discovered not only compatibility but also warmth, friendship and understanding. An arranged meeting soon turned into countless conversations, shared dreams and a growing bond. Sometimes destiny writes stories in the most unexpected ways — and this is where our story truly began.

A Week of Celebrations

From traditional ceremonies to modern celebrations, our wedding week is designed to blend cultural traditions with personal touches. Each event represents a milestone in our relationship and a chance to share special moments with family and friends.

Roka

An intimate family gathering where traditions are honored. We celebrate the official bond between our families with joy and elegance.

Engagement

The beginning of our journey together. A beautiful ceremony where our families unite and we exchange tokens of our commitment.

Sangeet

A night of music, dance, and celebration. Friends and family gather to perform traditional songs and dances that honor both families.

Haldi

Vibrant colors, laughter, and blessings. This pre-wedding ritual is filled with joy as family and friends bless us with turmeric and flowers.

Wedding Ceremony

The most important day of our lives! We exchange vows under the watchful eyes of loved ones and embark on our journey as husband and wife.

Reception

An evening of celebration, elegant dining, and dancing. We celebrate with our extended family and friends in joy and gratitude.
