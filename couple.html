Meet the Groom & Bride

A journey that begins with two lives shaped by their own dreams and paths, brought together by fate and family—slowly turning an arranged beginning into a bond filled with understanding, warmth, and love. What starts as a meeting of two worlds is blossoming into a lifetime of shared moments, quiet companionship, and a story they are choosing to write together, every single day.

Vishal

Childhood: Growing up, Vishal was quiet yet deeply curious—the youngest of the siblings, often taking on more than he could handle, and still managing it all with effortless finesse. He found joy in playing cricket and had a natural love for writing, expressing himself in ways words quietly allowed.

Life Events: Vishal’s journey is unfolding through new cities and experiences, shaped by his roots in engineering and a quiet drive to keep growing. He is constantly picking up new hobbies, exploring places that spark his curiosity, and finding simple joy in cooking and writing. There’s a calm rhythm to the way he moves through life—embracing change, collecting moments, and discovering himself a little more with each step, while knowing that the best parts of his story are meant to be shared.

Simoni

Childhood: Simoni was a whirlwind of energy and creativity. She is bold and full of drive—someone who naturally takes charge and ensures things get done. As an elder sister, she is caring and dependable, always looking out for her siblings while being the strong, guiding presence they rely on.

Life Events: Simoni’s journey is gently unfolding in the world of academics, where she is growing as a devoted research scholar and finding quiet joy in teaching. She is constantly exploring, learning, and sharing little sparks of knowledge that light up the people around her. There’s a certain ease in the way she balances depth with warmth—getting lost in ideas one moment, and effortlessly bringing them to life the next. And somewhere along the way, she’s still discovering that the most beautiful parts of her story are yet to come.
